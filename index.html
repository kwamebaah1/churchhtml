Welcome Message

I am delighted to warmly welcome you to our homepage. You have come to the right place at the right time. Here, we are very passionate about your eternal destiny and committed to your spiritual welfare. This is a website that has a lot to offer you with unlimited access to life-transforming, systematic and expository teaching of the word of God and resources specifically tailored to meet your own spiritual needs; deepen your walk with God and add value to your entire life.
Our numerous resources are carefully prepared with you in mind- to satisfy your spiritual hunger; enhance your spiritual growth and; equip you for divine service, all-around success, and victory over the challenges of life. As you continue to explore and enjoy the riches of grace uncovered through our numerous materials and online resources, may the Lord continue to richly bless you (Amen).

~ Rev. Dr. Patrick Yankyera

Our Services

English Service

7:00 - 9:30am
The first service of the day. Begins right after the Sunday School class at 7am. A purely english service for our members who can't cope with the local dialect

Twi Service

10:00 - 12:30am
The second service of the day. Begins right after the Sunday School class at 10am. A twi service for our members who find the english language undecipherable

Joint Service

9:30 - 12:30am
On the first Sunday of every month we have only one service which is our joint service. It's the day we dine with the Lord. We try our best to combine both the English and the local dialect in this service.
